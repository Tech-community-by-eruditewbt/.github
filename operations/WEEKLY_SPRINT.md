# Weekly Sprint (Template)
*Powered by EruditeWBT*

## Sprint Goal (One Sentence)

## Sprint Scope
- What we will ship
- What we will not do

## Daily Check-in (5 minutes)
- Yesterday: shipped / learned
- Today: next step
- Blockers: specific

## End of Week Demo Day
Each builder posts:
- demo link (video/GIF/screenshot)
- repo link
- 5 bullets: what changed this week
- next week goal

## Review Rules
- Be precise
- Give 1–3 actionable improvements
- Praise shipped work (not intentions)
