# Governance (v0)
*Powered by EruditeWBT*

This community is designed to scale without collapsing into chaos.

## Decision Model
- **Culture rules** are non-negotiable (see `RULES.md`).
- **Technical standards** evolve through shipped examples + review.
- **New tracks/branches** are added when:
  - at least 3 members are actively building in that area
  - there is a clear “trunk prerequisites” list
  - there is a starter project path

## Moderation
- Moderators enforce `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` and `RULES.md`.
- Moderation decisions are logged internally.

## Mentorship
- Mentors are Builders+ with a track record of shipping and reviewing.
- Mentors run:
  - office hours
  - project reviews
  - sprint check-ins

## Progression
- Roles are earned through shipped work.
- “Rank” without proof doesn’t exist here.
