# Code of Conduct
*Powered by EruditeWBT*

We are here to build. That requires trust.

## We Expect
- Respectful communication
- Constructive technical critique
- Good-faith collaboration
- Credit where it’s due

## We Do Not Allow
- Harassment, hate speech, or intimidation
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Plagiarism or misrepresenting work
- Persistent bad faith behavior

## Reporting
Report issues to moderators with:
- what happened
- where (link/screenshot)
- who was involved
- what outcome you want

Enforcement actions include warnings, role changes, temporary mutes, or removal.
