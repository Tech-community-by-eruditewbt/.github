# Community Rules
*Powered by EruditeWBT*

## The Six Rules
1. No passive learning. Build.
2. Ship weekly (even small).
3. Document everything important.
4. Teach forward (share what you learn).
5. Real problems only (projects must have a user, even if it’s you).
6. No fake expertise (ask questions, show work).

## Enforcement (Simple)
- Repeated non-participation → moved to “Observer” role.
- Disrespect, harassment, or bad faith → removal.
